About the host

Jhanvi Tiwari

Jhanvi Tiwari is the creator and host of The Jhanvi Tiwari Show, a long-form interview podcast that sits at the intersection of power, ambition, and the unwritten rules of professional life.

Before the podcast, Jhanvi spent four years running operations and marketing at a hospital, an experience that gave her a front-row seat to how institutions actually work, who holds power inside them, and what it takes to navigate rooms not built for you.

That institutional lens is at the heart of every conversation on the show. She talks to women who have built significant careers in arts, business, writing, and media, not just about what they achieved, but how they moved, negotiated, positioned themselves, and survived.

She is also a screenwriter and the designer behind Sorrow, a luxury Chikankari label currently in development.
